## 2. Description of Services

LuminoLearn Academy provides structured educational programs in Mathematics, Language & Literacy, and Computer Science for children and youth ages 9–17. Our Services include:

- **LuminoStart™:** A 4-week assessment and placement program that evaluates a Student's current level, learning style, and pace, resulting in a personalized learning plan and placement recommendation.
- **LuminoCore™:** A 12-week certified learning program with two sessions per week, including guided instruction, personalized practice, projects, teacher feedback, and a certificate of completion.
- **LuminoPath™:** Extended learning bundles of multiple LuminoCore™ cycles (6, 9, or 12 months), offering ongoing personalization, priority scheduling, and regular progress reports.
- **LuminoPro:** Professional development services for schools, teachers, and adult learners. Separate terms may apply.
- **Free Discovery Calls:** Complimentary 30-minute consultations to discuss a child's learning needs and program fit.
- **Learning Platform:** A secure online environment where Students access lesson materials, assignments, and progress information, managed by LuminoLearn staff.

### 4.1 Discovery Call

Families are encouraged to begin with a free discovery call. This is a 30-minute conversation to discuss your child's needs and determine whether LuminoLearn is a good fit. There is no obligation to enroll.

To enroll a child, Parents must complete an enrollment form providing the required information (parent contact details, child's name, age group, subject, and program selection). Submission of an enrollment form is a request, not a confirmed enrollment.

Upon confirmation and receipt of payment, our team creates the Student's account and provides login credentials to the Parent by email. **Parents and Students do not create their own accounts.**

LuminoLearn reserves the right to decline an enrollment request if we determine that our programs are not an appropriate fit for a Student's needs, if enrollment capacity has been reached, or for other reasonable educational or operational reasons. ## 6. Refund and Cancellation Policy

### 6.1 LuminoStart™

LuminoStart™ fees are non-refundable once the first session has been delivered, as the service involves individualized assessment work that begins immediately. If LuminoLearn cancels LuminoStart™ before any sessions are delivered, a full refund will be issued.

### 6.2 LuminoCore™

- **Before the program begins:** Full refund if cancellation is requested at least 7 days before the first session.
- **Within the first 2 weeks:** A prorated refund for remaining weeks, minus a $100 administrative fee.
- **After week 2:** No refund for the current cycle. The Student may complete the remaining sessions.

### 6.3 LuminoPath™

- **Before the program begins:** Full refund minus a $150 administrative fee.
- **During the program:** Refund is calculated on a per-cycle basis. Completed or in-progress cycles are non-refundable. Remaining future cycles are refundable minus a $100 administrative fee.

### 6.4 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or cancellation, contact us at lumino@luminolearn.org or +1 (437) 424-1380. Refunds are processed within 14 business days of approval via Interac e-Transfer.

### 6.5 LuminoLearn-Initiated Cancellations

If LuminoLearn cancels a program or session for any reason (including insufficient enrollment, instructor unavailability, or unforeseen circumstances), affected families will receive a full refund for cancelled sessions or be offered an equivalent replacement.

## 7. Student Conduct and Program Expectations

### 7.1 Attendance

Regular attendance is essential for learning. If a Student cannot attend a scheduled session, Parents should notify us at least 24 hours in advance. We make reasonable efforts to offer a make-up session, though this is subject to availability and not guaranteed.

### 7.2 Respectful Participation

Students are expected to participate respectfully in all sessions, treating teachers and fellow students with courtesy. LuminoLearn maintains a safe, supportive learning environment. Behavior that is persistently disruptive, disrespectful, or harmful to others may result in the following steps:

1. A conversation between the teacher and the Parent to address the behavior.
2. A temporary pause in sessions to allow the situation to be resolved.
3. In rare cases, discontinuation of enrollment, with a prorated refund for remaining sessions.

We approach behavioral concerns with care and always involve Parents before taking action.

### 7.3 Academic Integrity

Students are expected to submit their own work. Use of AI tools (such as ChatGPT or similar) is permitted only when explicitly authorized by the teacher and when the Student can explain their reasoning independently. Submitting AI-generated work as one's own without authorization is considered a breach of academic integrity.

When a concern arises, we address it as a learning opportunity — not a disciplinary event. We will discuss the situation with both the Student and Parent and use it to reinforce the principles of honest learning.
